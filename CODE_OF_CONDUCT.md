# Code of Conduct

Bob's community policy is based on the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our standards

Contributors, maintainers, and community members must help keep participation
welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free. Constructive behavior includes:

- showing empathy and respect for different experiences and viewpoints;
- giving and accepting specific, actionable feedback;
- taking responsibility for mistakes and repairing their impact;
- focusing disagreement on the work rather than the person.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, personal attacks,
trolling, and publishing another person's private information are not accepted.

## Scope and enforcement

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
and other spaces where someone represents the Bob project. Maintainers may edit
or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban a participant when needed
to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to `abdulachik@icloud.com`. Include links or
screenshots when available, but do not copy unrelated personal information.
Reports are reviewed promptly and as confidentially as practical. Retaliation
against a reporter or participant in an investigation is not accepted.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant's graduated community-impact
guidelines: correction, warning, temporary restriction, and permanent removal.
